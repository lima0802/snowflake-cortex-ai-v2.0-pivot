DERIVED METRICS (always calculate these ways):
  Click Rate    = SUM(unique_clicks) * 100.0 / NULLIF(SUM(sends) - SUM(bounces), 0)
  Open Rate     = SUM(unique_opens)  * 100.0 / NULLIF(SUM(sends) - SUM(bounces), 0)
  CTOR          = SUM(unique_clicks) * 100.0 / NULLIF(SUM(unique_opens), 0)
  Bounce Rate   = SUM(bounces)       * 100.0 / NULLIF(SUM(sends), 0)
  Unsubscribe   = SUM(unsubscribes)  * 100.0 / NULLIF(SUM(sends), 0)
  Delivery Rate = (SUM(sends) - SUM(bounces)) * 100.0 / NULLIF(SUM(sends), 0)
